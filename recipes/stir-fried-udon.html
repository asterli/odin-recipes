Stir-Fried Udon Noodles

My favorite dish from my po-po (grandma)!

Description

Boil a pot of hot water. Drop your pre-packaged udon noodles in the pot and wait 1-2 minutes until the noodles are cooked and chewy. Cut vegetables of your choice (I personally like cabbage and mushrooms). Prepare protein of choice (I prefer beef chunks). Create a noodle sauce by mixing together soy sauce, garlic, mirin, or any other flavors you want. Add the noodles, sauce, meat and vegetables into a large frying pan and start stir frying until the noodles are golden brown. Enjoy!
