Stir-Fried Udon Noodles

My favorite dish from my po-po (grandma)!

Description

Stir-fried udon is popular both in the Guangdong province of China (where my family is from) and in Japan (aka yakiudon). My grandma knows this is my favorite dish and loves to make it for me when I visit her. She cooks the Cantonese-style version and it leaves my stomach warm and happy.

Ingredients

  • Udon noodles
  • Cabbage
  • Mushrooms
  • Garlic and/or onions
  • Other vegetables of choice
  • Beef, chicken, pork, or any meat of choice
  • Soy sauce
  • Mirin
  • Oyster sauce
  • Salt and pepper

Steps

  1. Boil a pot of hot water.
  2. Drop your pre-packaged udon noodles in the pot and wait 1-2 minutes until the noodles are cooked and chewy.
  3. Cut vegetables of your choice (I personally like cabbage and mushrooms).
  4. Prepare protein of choice (I prefer beef chunks).
  5. Create a noodle sauce by mixing together soy sauce, garlic, mirin, or any other flavors you want.
  6. Add the noodles, sauce, meat and vegetables into a large frying pan and start stir frying until the noodles are golden brown.
  7. Enjoy!
