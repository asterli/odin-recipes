Clam Chowder

Extra creamy and in a bread bowl!

Description

I particularly like this dish because it reminds me of home in San Francisco. A creamy, thick chowder made with clams, potatoes, and bacon to warm you up especially on cold SF days!

Ingredients

  • Bacon
  • Butter
  • Garlic
  • Flour
  • Milk
  • Clams
  • Potatoes
  • Vegetable stock
  • Salt and pepper

Steps

  1. Heat a pot at medium and cook bacon until crispy
  2. Add butter and garlic until combined
  3. Whisk in flour until light brown
  4. Whisk in milk and vegetables stock
  5. Stir in potatoes
  6. Bring to a boil then reduce hit and. let sit for about 15 minutes
  7. Add clams and salt and pepper
  8. Enjoy!
